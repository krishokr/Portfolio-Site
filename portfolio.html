MANETTI-SHREM MUSEUM MOBILE APP: SLICE THE PIE

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Learn more.   Learn less.

Say you got to run a university. How much would you allocate to different sectors? Learn about your funding sources with a guessing game. You make your choices by inputting percentages of each section of a pie chart. See how well your choices match next to the real choices made by the Provost.

Responsibilities

I was responsible for all frontend work. This project uses React.js and D3.
